<p><em><strong>Note</strong>: Numerous valid examples can be credited, but to gain full credit (2 marks per service) candidates must identify relevant component of biome <strong>[1 mark]</strong> and outline the service it provides <strong>[1 mark]</strong> as in MPs above.</em><br/><em>Be careful only to credit “services” (maintenance/establishment of favourable conditions) and not “goods” (consumable/harvestable/physical products).</em></p>
