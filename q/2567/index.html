Two isolated point particles of mass 4M and 9M are separated by a distance 1 m. A point particle of mass M is placed a distance x  from the particle of mass 9M. The net gravitational force on M is zero.

What is x ?

 

A.    4 13 m

B.    2 5 m

C.    3 5 m

D.    9 13 m

Markscheme / solution

C
