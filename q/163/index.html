substitutes x=1 and attempts to solve for B and substitutes x=-12 and attempts to solve for A        (M1)

9=3B⇒B=3 ; 3A2=-32⇒A=-1

 

OR

forms A+B=2 and -A+2B=7 and attempts to solve for A and B        (M1)

 

THEN

A=-1 and B=3        A1A1

a.

uses the binomial expansion on either 31-x-1 or 1+2x-1       M1

3+3x+3x2-1-2x+4x2
