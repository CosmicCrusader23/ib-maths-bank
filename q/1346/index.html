valid approach to set up integration by substitution/inspection     (M1)

eg u = x 2 − 1 ,  d u = 2 x ,   ∫ 2 x e x 2 − 1 d x

correct expression     (A1)

eg 1 2 ∫ 2 x e x 2 − 1 d x ,   1 2 ∫ e u d u
