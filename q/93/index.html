Consider the equation z 4 + a z 3 + b z 2 + c z + d = 0 , where  a ,  b ,  c , d ∈ R and  z ∈ C .

Two of the roots of the equation are log26 and i 3 and the sum of all the roots is 3 + log23.

Show that 6 a + d + 12 = 0.
