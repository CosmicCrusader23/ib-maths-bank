A particle moves in a straight line such that its velocity, v ms-1, at time t seconds is given by

v=4t2-6t+9-2 sin4t, 0≤t≤1.

The particle’s acceleration is zero at t=T.

Find the value of T.

[2]
a.

Let s1 be the distance travelled by the particle from t=0 to t=T and let s2 be the distance travelled by the particle from t=T to t=1.

Show that s2>s1.

[3]
b.
Markscheme / solution
