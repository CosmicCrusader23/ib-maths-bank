Consider the function f , with derivative  f ′ ( x ) = 2 x 2 + 5 k x + 3 k 2 + 2 where  x ,  k ∈ R .

Show that the discriminant of  f ′ ( x ) is k 2 − 16 .

a.

Given that f is an increasing function, find all possible values of k .
