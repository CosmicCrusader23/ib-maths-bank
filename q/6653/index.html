Urea, (H2N)2CO, is excreted by mammals and can be used as a fertilizer.

Urea can also be made by the direct combination of ammonia and carbon dioxide gases.

2NH3(g) + CO2(g) ⇌ (H2N)2CO(g) + H2O(g)     ΔH < 0
