17M.2.AHL.TZ2.H_6

pestleAAHLPaper 217M· sl-1-5-intro-to-logssource ↗

Given that log 10 ( 1 2 2 ( p + 2 q ) ) = 1 2 ( log 10 p + log 10 q ) ,   p > 0 ,   q > 0 , find p in terms of q .

Markscheme / solution

* This question is from an exam for a previous syllabus, and may contain minor differences in marking or structure.

log 10 1 2 2 ( p + 2 q ) = 1 2 ( log 10 p + log 10 q )

log 10 1 2 2 ( p + 2 q ) = 1 2 log 10 p q      (M1)

log 10 1 2 2 ( p + 2 q ) = log 10 ( p q ) 1 2      (M1)

1 2 2 ( p + 2 q ) = ( p q ) 1 2      (A1)

( p + 2 q ) 2 = 8 p q

p 2 + 4 p q + 4 q 2 = 8 p q

p 2 − 4 p q + 4 q 2 = 0

( p − 2 q ) 2 = 0      M1

hence p = 2 q      A1
