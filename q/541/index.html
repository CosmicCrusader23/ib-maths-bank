evidence of choosing the sine rule     (M1)

eg a sin ⁡ A = b sin ⁡ B

eg x sin ⁡ 30 = 13 sin ⁡ 45 ,   13 sin ⁡ 30 sin ⁡ 45

sin ⁡ 30 = 1 2 ,   sin ⁡ 45 = 1 2     (A1)(A1)

correct working     A1

eg 1 2 × 13 1 2 ,   1 2 × 13 × 2 2 ,   13 × 1 2 × 2

correct answer     A1     N3
