<ul>

<p>Most candidates seemed to be familiar with AD/AS analysis and could use a diagram with relative ease. <p>Most candidates had a fairly good understanding of what fiscal policy is and how expansionary fiscal policy is achieved, but many were unable to explain specifically how it reduces unemployment. Many could evaluate fiscal policy in general, but not specifically as it relates to a reduction in unemployment.
