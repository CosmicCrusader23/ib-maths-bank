<p><strong>Value</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>It is a letter by Hitler himself, Fuhrer of Germany, after the Nazi–Soviet Pact was signed.</li>
<li>Its intention is to justify to Mussolini why the Pact was necessary and to gain Mussolini’s support.</li>
<li>It clearly identifies the reasons why Hitler wanted an agreement between Germany and the USSR.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Limitations</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>The source only gives an unofficial statement of policy while events were still unfolding.</li>
<li>The aim behind the letter is to gain Mussolini’s support and it may be merely exaggerating the reality of the situation.</li>
<li>It only offers a German perspective on the agreement and does not consider a Soviet perspective.</li>
</ul>
