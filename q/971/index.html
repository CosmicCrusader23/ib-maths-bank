The sum of an infinite geometric sequence is 33.25. The second term of the sequence is 7.98. Find the possible values of r .

Markscheme / solution

* This question is from an exam for a previous syllabus, and may contain minor differences in marking or structure.

correct substitution into formula for infinite geometric series      (A1)

eg      33.25 = u 1 1 − r

correct substitution into formula for u n (seen anywhere)      (A1)

eg      7.98 = u 1 r

attempt to express u 1 in terms of r (or vice-versa)      (M1)

eg      u 1 = 7.98 r ,    u 1 = 33.25 ( 1 − r ) ,    r = 7.98 u 1 ,    r = 33.25 − u 1 33.25

correct working      (A1)

eg    ( 7.98 r ) 1 − r = 33.25 ,    33.25 ( 1 − r ) = 7.98 r ,   (0.4, 19.95),   (0.6, 13.3),    u 1 1 − 7.98 u 1 = 33.25

r = 0.4 ( = 2 5 ) ,  r = 0.6 ( = 3 5 )         A1A1 N3
