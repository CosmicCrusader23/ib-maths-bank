Let  f ( x ) = e 2 sin ( π x 2 ) , for x > 0.

The k th maximum point on the graph of f has x-coordinate xk where  k ∈ Z + .

Given that xk + 1 = xk + a, find a.

[4]
a.

Hence find the value of n such that  ∑ k = 1 n x k = 861 .

b.
