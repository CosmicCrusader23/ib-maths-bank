The vectors a = ( 4 2 ) and b = ( k + 3 k ) are perpendicular to each other.

 

Find the value of k .

a.

[3]
b.
Markscheme / solution

evidence of scalar product     M1

eg a ∙ b, 4 ( k + 3 ) + 2 k

recognizing scalar product must be zero     (M1)

eg a ∙ b = 0 ,   4 k + 12 + 2 k = 0

correct working (must involve combining terms)     (A1)

eg   6 k + 12 , 6 k = − 12

k = − 2     A1     N2

[4 marks]

a.

attempt to substitute their value of k (seen anywhere)     (M1)

eg b = ( − 2 + 3 − 2 ) , 2b = ( 2 − 4 )

correct working     (A1)

eg ( 4 2 ) + ( 2 − 4 ) ,   ( 4 + 2 k + 6 2 + 2 k )

c = ( 6 − 2 )     A1     N2
