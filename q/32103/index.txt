Candidates may refer to Metternich having an important role in repressing various uprisings in 1820, 1830 and 1848 in the Italian states therefore influencing the growth of nationalism and liberalism. Candidates may refer to both being context specific and Metternich’s impact on these forces was stronger in the north. Candidates may argue that the uprisings in the Two Sicilies had very little to do with nationalism but sought liberal reform and the 1820 uprising there was suppressed with the aid of Austrian troops.
