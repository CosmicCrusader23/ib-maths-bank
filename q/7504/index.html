Markscheme / solution

Structure:
giant covalent/network covalent    [✔]

Bonding:
each carbon covalently bonded to 3 other carbons
OR
each bond has order of 1.5    [✔]

 

Note: Accept “cylindrical/tube shaped”.

Accept “has delocalized electrons” OR “has sp2 hybridization”.
