Let C =  ( − 2 4 1 7 ) and D = ( 5 2 − 1 a ) .

The 2 × 2 matrix Q is such that 3Q = 2C – D

Find Q.

[3]
a.

Find CD.

[4]
b.

Find D–1.

[2]
c.
