Let fx=mx2-2mx, where x∈ℝ and m∈ℝ. The line y=mx-9 meets the graph of f at exactly one point.

The function f can be expressed in the form fx=4x-px-q, where p, q∈ℝ.

The function f can also be expressed in the form fx=4x-h2+k, where h, k∈ℝ.

Show that m=4.
