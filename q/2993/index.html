A person with a weight of 600 N stands on a scale in an elevator.

What is the acceleration of the elevator when the scale reads 900 N?

A.  5.0 m s-2 downwards

B.  1.5 m s-2 downwards

C.  1.5 m s-2 upwards

D.  5.0 m s-2 upwards

Markscheme / solution

D
