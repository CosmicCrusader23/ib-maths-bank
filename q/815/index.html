Consider the points A(-2, 20), B(4, 6) and C(-14, 12). The line L passes through the point A and is perpendicular to [BC].

Find the equation of L.

[3]
a.

The line L passes through the point (k, 2).

Find the value of k.

[2]
b.
Markscheme / solution

mBC=12-6-14-4 =-13        (A1)

finding mL=-1mBC using their mBC        (M1)

mL=3

y-20=3x+2,  y=3x+26        A1

 

Note: Do not accept L=3x+26

 

[3 marks]

a.

substituting (k, 2) into their L        (M1)

2-20=3k+2  OR  2=3k+26

k=-8        A1

 

[2 marks]
