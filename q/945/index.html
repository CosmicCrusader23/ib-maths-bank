The random variable X has the Poisson distribution Po ( m ) . Given that P ( X > 0 ) = 3 4 , find the value of m in the form ln ⁡ a where a is an integer.

⇒ 1 − e − m = 3 4 or equivalent     A1

⇒ m = ln ⁡ 4      A1

a.

= 1 − e − 2 ln ⁡ 4 − e − 2 ln ⁡ 4 × 2 ln ⁡ 4      A1
