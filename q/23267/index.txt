<p>Like an ecosystem, soil is an open system with inputs and outputs;<br/>(inputs of) <em>eg</em> water/O<sub>2</sub> (and outputs of) <em>eg</em> CO<sub>2</sub>/nitrogen;<br/>...and storages and flows/processes;<br/>(storages of) <em>eg</em> nitrates/water (and flows of) <em>eg</em> leaching/decomposition;<br/>like an ecosystem, soil is a community of living/biotic and abiotic elements;<br/>(biotic) <em>eg</em> bacteria/fungi/earthworms (and abiotic) <em>eg</em> clay/sand/silt/water/heat;<br/>...with the many complex interactions/interrelationships/<em>eg</em> mineral cycling/leguminous plants;<br/>interacts with/supports other systems/<em>eg</em> vegetation growth, animal movement/burrowing/human development;</p>
<p><em>Award marks as above for identifying relevant common features, and also for giving examples (provided it is clear what is being exemplified).</em></p>
<div class="question_part_label">a.</div>
</div><div class="question" style="padding-left: 20px;">
<p>in both cycles combustion (of forests/fossil fuels) increases concentration of oxides in atmosphere;<br/>in both cycles deforestation/agriculture/SDW lead to decomposition that also releases oxides;<br/>...but carbon dioxide released (by respiration) into atmosphere/(whereas) nitrous oxides are released into soil water (by nitrification);<br/>both oxides will increase impact of global warming/climate change;<br/>...but NOx to a smaller degree;<br/>both oxides result in the acidification of water/aquatic bodies;<br/>...but only NOx may cause acid deposition/acidify soils;<br/>deforestation removes organic storages of both N and C (stored in plant biomass);<br/>…and reduces absorption of C from atmosphere (via photosynthesis) (but not N);<br/>…causes soil erosion which reduces inorganic N storages in soil (but not C);<br/>use of inorganic fertilisers increases N in soil (but not C);<br/>…and run-off may cause excessive inorganic N in aquatic systems (but not C);<br/>pesticide/herbicide use in agriculture might kill organisms thus reducing both C and N organic storages (stored in their biomass);<br/>… thus reducing nitrification/denitrification/decomposition process/(whereas) effect on C cycle is limited to reducing respiration by soil animals;<br/>extraction of oil/coal/gas reduces underground (ancient) C storages/transfers C storages on surface (for human use)/(whereas) effect to N cycle is limited to a few organic compounds/aromatics found in oil;</p>
<p><em>Award 5 max if only similarities or only differences are identified.</em></p>
