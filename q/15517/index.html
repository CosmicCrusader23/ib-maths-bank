In humans, male pattern baldness is caused by a recessive sex-linked gene found only on the X chromosome.

If a father who does not have male pattern baldness and a mother who is a carrier for it have a child, what is the probability that the child will develop male pattern baldness in adulthood?

A. 0 %

B. 25 %

C. 50 %

D. 75 %

Markscheme / solution

B
