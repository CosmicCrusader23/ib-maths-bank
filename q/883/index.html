EXM.1.AHL.TZ0.14

It is believed that two variables, v  and  w  are related by the equation  v = k w n , where  k , n ∈ R .  Experimental values of  v  and  w  are obtained. A graph of  ln v against  ln w  shows a straight line passing through (−1.7, 4.3) and (7.1, 17.5).

Find the value of  k  and of  n . 

Markscheme / solution

ln v = n ln w + ln k        M1A1

gradient  = 17.5 − 4.3 7.1 + 1.7   ( = 1.5 )        M1

n = 1.5             A1

y -intercept  = 1.5 × 1.7 + 4.3   ( = 6.85 )        M1

k = e 6.85 = 944        M1A1
