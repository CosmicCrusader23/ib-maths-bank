Suppose that zw ∈ℤ.

Find the modulus of zw.

[1]
a.

Find the argument of zw in terms of k.

[2]
b.

Find the minimum value of k.

[3]
c.i.

For the value of k found in part (i), find the value of zw.

[1]
c.ii.
