Two wave generators, placed at position P and position Q, produce water waves with a wavelength of 4.0 cm. Each generator, operating alone, will produce a wave oscillating with an amplitude of 3.0 cm at position R. PR is 42 cm and RQ is 60 cm.

Both wave generators now operate together in phase. What is the amplitude of the resulting wave at R?


A.  9 cm

B.  6 cm

C.  3 cm

D.  zero

Markscheme / solution

D
