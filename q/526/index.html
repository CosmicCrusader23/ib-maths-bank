Let f ( x ) = 6 x − 1 2 x + 3 , for x ≠ − 3 2 .

For the graph of f , find the y -intercept.

[2]
a.i.

Hence or otherwise, write down lim x → ∞ ⁡ ( 6 x − 1 2 x + 3 ) .
